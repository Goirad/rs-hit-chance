Note on Equipment penalty

Enter 1.5x the total armour you have equipped of the style your weapon is strong against, and subtract 0.8x the total armour you have equipped of style your weapon is weak against

e.g if using a bow, enter 1.5x total magic armour and 0.8x total melee armour you have equipped

You can also enter the "hybrid nerf" stat from Equipment interface: hover over accuracy stat in game, once you have equipped the gear you intend to use

More Information

If you need help using this calculator, don't hesitate to ask in the PvME Discord server!

You can also join the RS Math server where you can be notified of updates, post suggestions, report errors, or receive additional help.

This spreadsheet does not automatically account for defence level drains from things like the Guthix Staff or Statius' Warhammer special attacks. Manually enter these using the Additional defence drain field. Bear in mind some monsters are immune to defence level drains e.g. Araxxor.
